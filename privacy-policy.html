Privacy Policy

PaintTopia

This Privacy Policy explains how PaintTopia collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use the app.

Effective date: March 19, 2026 Package: com.boredjejemon.paintopia Support: boredjejemonph@gmail.com

1. Overview

PaintTopia is a pixel-art guide and drawing helper app. Users can import images, generate pixel previews, create step-by-step color guides, save local guide files, export guide GIFs, and use a floating overlay guide for tracing workflows.

PaintTopia does not require user account registration to use its core features.

2. Information We Process

Images you choose

When you upload or import an image, the app processes that image locally on your device to generate pixel-art previews, guide steps, saved guides, and GIF exports.

Locally stored guide data

PaintTopia can store generated guide data, preview images, source images, and guide metadata locally on your device so that you can reopen previous work later.

Support emails

If you use the in-app report/contact feature, PaintTopia opens your email app with a prefilled template. The email you send is handled by your chosen email provider.

3. Information Collected by Third Parties

PaintTopia uses Google AdMob to display ads. As part of ad delivery, Google and its partners may collect or process certain device, advertising, diagnostics, and app interaction data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

To learn more about how Google handles data for ads, please review Google's own policies and privacy information.

4. How We Use Information

  • Generate pixel-art previews and guide steps.
  • Save and restore local artwork progress.
  • Export guide animations as GIF files.
  • Enable floating overlay guide features.
  • Support in-app ads through AdMob.
  • Help users contact support through their mail app.

5. Permissions Used

Internet and network state

PaintTopia uses network-related permissions for ad delivery and connectivity handling.

Draw over other apps

PaintTopia includes an optional floating overlay guide. This feature requires draw-over-apps permission and is only used if the user explicitly enables and launches the overlay guide.

6. Data Storage

User-generated guide files, preview files, exported GIFs, and saved source images are stored locally on the device unless the user manually shares or exports them elsewhere.

PaintTopia does not currently operate its own cloud account system for syncing user artwork.

7. Data Sharing

PaintTopia does not sell your personal images or saved guide files. However, third-party ad services such as AdMob may process data needed to serve ads according to their own terms.

8. Children's Privacy

PaintTopia is not designed specifically for children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe a child has provided information through the app in a way that concerns you, please contact us.

9. Your Choices

  • You can uninstall the app at any time.
  • You can clear app storage to remove locally saved guides and app data.
  • You can choose whether to grant draw-over-apps permission for the overlay guide.
  • You can choose whether to use the in-app support email flow.
