How it works

Three steps from photo to finished pixel art.

Step 01

Import

Pick any photo from your gallery. Choose your grid size — the app pixelates it to the exact canvas resolution.

→
Step 02

Generate Guide

Pixels are grouped by color into numbered steps so you paint one shade at a time, no guesswork needed.

→
Step 03

Trace

Float the overlay on top of any game or drawing app. Lock it to pass-through mode while you paint each pixel.

Features

Everything you need to go from a photo to finished pixel art.

🖼️

Smart Pixelation

Import any photo and choose your grid precision — the app generates an accurate pixel-art preview locked to your canvas size.

🎨

Color Guide Steps

Pixels grouped by color into numbered steps. Paint one shade at a time with no missed spots and no confusion.

🔲

Floating Overlay

A draggable, resizable floating panel that sits on top of any app. Lock it to transparent pass-through while you paint.

📐

Grid & Coordinates

Toggle a reference grid with optional pixel coordinate labels so every dot lands in exactly the right place.

🎞️

GIF Export

Export your guide as a compact animated GIF to share with friends or keep as an offline reference.

💾

Local First

All guides and previews are saved on your device. No account, no cloud — your art stays yours.
