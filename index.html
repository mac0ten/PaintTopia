How it works

Three steps from photo to finished pixel art.

Step 01

Import

Pick any photo from your gallery. Choose your grid size and crop mode — the app pixelates it to the exact canvas resolution using the Heartopia 125-color palette.

→
Step 02

Generate Guide

Pixels are grouped by color into numbered steps so you paint one shade at a time. Export as an animated GIF or navigate step by step on device.

→
Step 03

Trace with Overlay

Float the guide overlay on top of Heartopia or any drawing app. Lock it to pass-through mode and trace each pixel without leaving the game.

Features

Everything you need to go from a photo to finished pixel art.

image

Smart Pixelation

Import any photo and choose your grid precision (Small to XL). The app converts it to the exact canvas resolution using the Heartopia 125-color palette.

palette

Color Guide Steps

Pixels are grouped by color across 13 hue groups and 5–10 tone variants. Paint one shade at a time — no missed spots, no guesswork.

layers

Floating Overlay

A draggable, resizable guide panel that floats on top of any app. Lock it to pass-through mode so it doesn't interfere while you paint.

grid_on

Grid & Coordinates

Toggle a reference grid with pixel coordinate labels so every dot lands in exactly the right place on your canvas.

gif

GIF Export

Export your guide as a compact animated GIF to share with friends or use as an offline step-by-step reference.

save

Local First

All guides, previews, and converted images are saved on your device. No account, no cloud — your art stays private.
